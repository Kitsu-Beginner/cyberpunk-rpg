Physical Attributes

Physical attributes define your character's bodily capabilities, such as their raw power, speed, and toughness. They are the foundation for most physical actions.

They build the base for a almost all dicepools and also function as your total health. Each point of physical damage lowers one physical attribute by one until it is healed.

Stamina

Stamina represents your total cardiovascular fitness and health. If your stamina falls to 0, your character counts as clinically dead.

Damage to the torso or by toxins usually counts as damage to stamina and represent damage to inner organs or arteries. Stamina directly affects:

  • The weight you can carry without penalty
  • Dice Pools like athletics, survival etc.

Strength

Strength represents the raw physical power of your major upper body muscles. If you strength falls to 0 you are unable to do most actions, including attacks

Damage to arms or major muscles usually count as damage to strength. Strength directly affects:

  • The weight you can carry without penalty
  • Your unarmed damage (halved and rounded up)
  • A bonus to your dicepool when firing full auto (optional)
  • Dice Pools like Close Combat, Athletics, Heavy Weapons etc.

Agility

Agility is your overall speed and flexibility, representing your leg muscles as well as flexibility of your joints. If you Agility falls to 0, you can only move by a slow crawl.

Damage to your legs and joints usually counts as damage to Agility. Agility directly affects:

  • Your Defense Pool (Agility+Intuition)
  • Your Speed in metres per combat round (Agility+Reaction)/2
  • Dice Pools like Close Combat

Reaction

Your neural speed and reflex loops. How fast you can react to real world stimuli. If you Reaction falls to 0, you fall into a coma.

Damage to your head and nervous system usually counts as damage to Reaction. Reaction directly affects:

  • Your Speed in metres per combat round (Agility+Reaction)/2
  • Your Initiative (Reaction+Intuition)
  • Dice Pools like Firearms, Drive or Piloting

Mental Attributes

Mental attributes represent the cognitive, emotional, and instinctive processes that drive perception, decision-making, and control

They build the base for almost all dicepools and also function as your stun damage monitor. Each point of stun damage lowers one mental attribute by one unitl recoverd.

Intuition

Your instincts an gut-level processing. What your brain concludes before you can weigh in consciously. If Intuition falls to 0, you black out for a bit.

Intuition directly affects:

  • Your Defense Pool (Agility+Intuition)
  • Your Initiative (Reaction+Intuition)
  • Dice Pools like Heavy Weapons

Logic

The ability of methodically and conscious thinking, Cold calculation, coding and math are examples. If Logic falls to 0 you fall into a short stupor.

Logic directly affects:

  • Plenty of technical dicepools

Composure

Your ability to keep your nerves and clean head under pressure. If Composure falls to 0 you freeze for a moment.

Composure directly affects:

  • Dice Pools like Firearms or Stealth

Special Attributes

These attributes are outliers in some way

Humanity

Humanity is the measure of how much of you is still human. Empathy, social instincts and the "soul". Installing Cyberware has a "cost" in humanity.

Humanity directly affects:

  • Most notably the amount of magic drain that is converted from physical to stun damage
  • Dice Pools like Insight or Carouse
  • as a Dice Pool modifier to some effects and spells

Skillbase

The Dicepool of every skill is calculated by adding the skill to a basevalue, which is the mean of 2 Attributes.

For example the Firearms skill has the base composure_reaction, which is the rounded up mean value.

So with values of 3 and 4 you get a base of 4 (3.5 rounded up) and add your Firearms skill for your total Dice Pool

Other

These are other values that are not calculated of you main attributes

Natural Armor

Natural Armor is gained by Cyberware or Spells and adds its armor rating to the worn armor

Auto Bonus

Auto Bonus is gained through high Strength or special cyberware and adds its rating to all full auto attacks made with firearms

Secondary Attributes

These are values that are simply calculated from your standard attributes

Defense Pool

Defense Pool is your (Agility + Intuition) and is rolled as defense against almost all attacks

In some circumstances you only roll half of your Defense Pool, notably when flanked or when defending against AOE Attacks

Speed

Speed is calculated by (Agility + Reaction)/2 and dtermines the number of metres you can move per Action in a combat round.

Initiative

Initiative is calculated as (Reaction + Intuition) and determines the order of combat

Carry Weight

It is calculated as (10 + (Strength + Stamina)) and determines the weight in kg a character can carry without penalty.

The skill of using handheld weapons like knives, swords, and clubs in close quarters.

Specializations: Unarmed, Melee, Gunjitsu

Dodge

Allows the player to spend 1 AP to add the dodge rating to the defense roll

Specializations: Melee, Ranged, AOE

Athletics

stamina/strength

Represents physical fitness, covering actions like swimming, climbing, and lifting

Specializations: Running, Swimming, Lifting

Stealth

agility/composure

The skill of remaining unseen and unheard, moving silently, and hiding.

Specializations: Shadowing, Avoiding, Inflitration

Knowledge of outdoor survival, including tracking, foraging, and navigating wilderness.

Specializations: Urban, Wilderness, Desert
