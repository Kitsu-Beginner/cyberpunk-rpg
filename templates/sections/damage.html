Damage & Trauma

Physical damage is visceral and immediate. Damage is subtracted directly from the character’s Physical Attributes (Stamina, Strength, Agility, Reaction). This reduction immediately lowers any derived statistics (e.g., lower Agility reduces Defense Pool; lower Reaction reduces Initiative).

Critical System Failure (0 Value)

If an attribute is reduced to 0, the character suffers a catastrophic failure of that body system.

Attribute Status Effect
Stamina CLINICAL DEATH Trauma to vital organs/arteries. Character dies permanently within minutes without emergency resuscitation.
Strength LIMB FAILURE Muscular/Structural collapse. Arms are useless. Character cannot perform physical attacks.
Agility CRIPPLED Legs/Tendons destroyed. Max Speed = 1 (Crawling). Character is always considered Flanked (Defense Pool halved).
Reaction INCAPACITATED Neural/Spinal trauma. Character is unconscious or paralyzed and cannot take actions.

Rule 1: System Shock

Whenever an attribute hits 0, the traumatic shock causes the character to immediately suffer 1 additional point of Stamina damage. This usually causes bleeding as described below.

Rule 2: Hemorrhage (Bleeding)

Any kinetic attack (bullets, blades) that deals Stamina damage as well as cascade damage causes Bleeding.

  • Stage 1: Suffer 1 Stamina Dmg every 12 Rounds.
  • Stage 2: If hit again while bleeding, the rate worsens to 1 Stamina Dmg every 6 Rounds.

Stun & Mental Stress

Just like physical damage, Stun damage is subtracted directly from the character’s Mental Attributes (Intuition, Logic, Composure). For every point of damage received, the player must lower one Mental Attribute by 1.

Mental Breakdown (0 Value)

If any mental attribute is reduced to 0, the character suffers a mental breakdown.

  • Immediate Effect: The character loses all remaining actions for the current combat round.
  • Duration: The character cannot act again until ALL mental attributes have been restored to a value of at least 1.

Specific Trauma Effects

Attribute Status Effect
Intuition DISORIENTED The character is blinded or suffers a sensory blackout. They lose spatial awareness.
Logic CONFUSED Cognitive collapse. The character is bewildered and unable to process information or formulate plans.
Composure SHOCK Psychological paralysis. The character freezes in a state of shock or panic and cannot move.

Recovery

Regeneration Passive Effect

At the start of every Combat Round, the character automatically regenerates 1 point to a mental attribute of their choice.
